Plant

A plant is a living organism that typically grows in the ground, has leaves, and produces its own food through photosynthesis. Plants are essential for life on Earth as they provide oxygen, food, and habitat for many organisms.

Types of Plants

  • Flowering Plants

    These plants produce flowers and seeds. Examples include roses, sunflowers, and tulips.

  • Non-Flowering Plants

    These plants do not produce flowers. Examples include ferns, mosses, and conifers.

  • Succulents

    These plants have thick, fleshy tissues that store water. Examples include cacti and aloe vera.

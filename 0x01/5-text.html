Briards

a heart wrapped in fur

The Briard is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herding dog and a guard dog. They are known for their long, shaggy coat and their loyalty to their owners.

Breed History

The Briard which is believed to have originated in France, was initially used for herding and guarding livestock.the breed was used by the French army during World War I as a messenger dog and a search and rescue dog. Today, Briards are popular as family pets and are also used in various dog sports and activities.
